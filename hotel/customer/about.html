About Gloria Hotel

Located just inside the historic Jaffa Gate in the heart of Jerusalem's Old City, the Gloria Hotel is a family-owned establishment that has proudly served guests since the 1950s. With a prime location in the Christian Quarter, the hotel offers unrivaled access to religious, cultural, and historical landmarks.

Over the years, the hotel has undergone major renovations to blend traditional hospitality with modern comforts. Guests can enjoy the unique experience of staying within the ancient city walls while enjoying fully equipped, air-conditioned rooms with en-suite bathrooms, satellite television, and high-speed internet.

Our dedicated staff has decades of experience in hospitality and prides itself on providing impeccable service and personalized attention to each guest. Whether you're a solo traveler, couple, family, or part of a group tour, you'll find a welcoming home at the Gloria Hotel.
